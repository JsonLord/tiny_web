## Key Evidence: The Onboarding Barrier

*   **Evidence**: OneTrust banner covers ~40% of the viewport on load.
*   **Principle**: Low Barrier to Entry.
*   **Consequence**: Users (like Oscar) feel "interrogated" before they see value.
*   **Risk**: High bounce rate for first-time professional visitors.

:::card
### Recommendation
Use a **non-blocking** footer-based consent banner to allow immediate page scanning.
:::
